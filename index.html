Equipment Workouts

Dumbbell Bench Press

Chest Intermediate

Lie on bench with dumbbells at shoulder width. Press weights up until arms are extended, then lower slowly.

  • 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • 60-90 sec rest between sets
  • Focus on controlled movement

Barbell Squat

Legs Advanced

Stand with barbell across upper back, feet shoulder-width apart. Lower hips until thighs are parallel to floor, then drive up.

  • 4 sets of 6-10 reps
  • 2-3 min rest between sets
  • Keep chest up and core tight

Lat Pulldown

Back Beginner

Sit at pulldown station, grip bar wider than shoulders. Pull bar down to chest, then slowly return.

  • 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • 60 sec rest between sets
  • Engage back muscles, not arms
